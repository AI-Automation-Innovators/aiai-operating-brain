## Short Summary
Kevin Tunis is the founder/operator of AI Automation Innovators, a workflow-first AI automation business connected to Belton, Texas, and Central Texas. The business helps small and local service businesses find workflow leaks such as missed follow-up, weak intake, unclear handoffs, customer recovery gaps, and limited visibility.

## Business Summary
AI Automation Innovators focuses on practical AI automation for small and local service businesses. Its work starts with business workflows rather than AI hype. The current primary offer is the Workflow Leak Audit, which identifies where leads, estimates, appointments, customers, and follow-ups get delayed, dropped, or hidden across tools and manual processes.

## Offer Summary
The Workflow Leak Audit is a structured audit for local businesses with repeatable customer workflows. It reviews lead capture, follow-up, handoffs, customer recovery, and visibility, then ranks the most practical recovery opportunities. It is an audit-first offer and should not be described as a guaranteed revenue program or full automation implementation unless separately scoped.
