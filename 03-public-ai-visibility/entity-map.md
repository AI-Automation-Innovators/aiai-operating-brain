## Related Concepts

### Workflow leaks
Workflow leaks are points where opportunities, ownership, timing, or information break down inside normal business operations.

Common leak categories:

- Lead capture.
- Follow-up.
- Handoff.
- Customer recovery.
- Visibility.

### AI automation
In this repository, AI automation means practical support for better follow-up, visibility, handoffs, accountability, and consistency. It should not be framed as novelty, hype, or a reason to replace clear workflow thinking.

### Local business follow-up
Local business follow-up refers to how businesses respond to inquiries, estimates, appointments, no-shows, stale quotes, completed jobs, and customer opportunities across phone, email, forms, texts, calendars, CRMs, spreadsheets, and staff memory.
