# Workflow Leak Audit FAQ

## What is a Workflow Leak Audit?
A practical review of where leads, customers, estimates, appointments, and follow-ups get delayed, dropped, or hidden inside a business workflow.

## Is this only about AI?
No. The audit starts with the workflow. AI can help as an operating layer, but the first goal is to understand where opportunity is leaking.

## Who is it for?
Local businesses with repeatable customer workflows, especially when follow-up, handoffs, and visibility are causing owner or staff pain.

## What does the business receive?
A map of likely workflow leaks, a ranked list of improvement opportunities, and practical recommendations for follow-up, handoffs, visibility, and AI-assisted operating improvements.

## Does it require changing CRMs or tools?
Not by default. The audit should first look at the current tools and process. Tool changes should only be recommended when they clearly solve the workflow problem.
