# FAQ

## What is a Workflow Leak Audit?
A practical review of where leads, quotes, follow-up, tasks, customer communication, or revenue opportunities may be leaking from the business because the workflow is manual, inconsistent, untracked, or dependent on memory.

## Is this an AI audit?
No. The audit is not a pitch for AI tools. It finds workflow gaps first. AI may become the operating layer behind a fix if it helps the business follow up, track, or report more consistently.

## Who is this for?
Local service businesses, home service companies, real estate teams, local professional services, and small businesses in Belton, Temple, Salado, Killeen, Harker Heights, and Central Texas.

## What kinds of leaks do you look for?
Missed calls, missed website inquiries, slow email response, forgotten quotes, no follow-up after estimates, manual intake gaps, untracked lead sources, CRM gaps, dormant customers, and lack of weekly visibility into open opportunities.

## Do I need a CRM?
No. A CRM can help, but the audit can also review spreadsheets, inboxes, call logs, forms, calendars, and manual processes.

## Do you replace my staff?
No. The goal is to help staff and owners stop relying on memory for repeatable follow-up and admin work.

## What happens after the audit?
You receive a summary of leaks, a ranked opportunity list, quick-win recommendations, and a suggested path for the first workflow improvement. If there is a fit, an optional implementation proposal may follow.

## How long does it take?
The audit should be kept practical and focused. The exact timeline depends on how many workflows, lead sources, quotes, and customer records need to be reviewed.

## Is this only for Belton businesses?
No. Belton is a primary local focus, but the offer also fits businesses in Temple, Salado, Killeen, Harker Heights, and Central Texas.

## What does AI Automation Innovators do?
AI Automation Innovators helps local businesses improve follow-up, quote recovery, missed-opportunity workflows, customer communication, and operational visibility. AI is used when it supports a practical business outcome.
