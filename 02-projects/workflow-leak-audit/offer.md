# Workflow Leak Audit Offer

## Offer Summary
The Workflow Leak Audit helps local businesses identify where leads, quotes, follow-up, tasks, customer communication, and revenue opportunities are leaking from their current workflow.

This is not a pitch for AI tools. The audit finds workflow gaps first, then recommends practical fixes.

## Core Promise
Find missed opportunities already inside the business and show the simplest path to recover them through better follow-up, tracking, visibility, and practical automation.

## Problems It Solves
- Leads are coming in, but follow-up is inconsistent.
- Quotes are sent, but not recovered.
- Staff are too busy to remember every next step.
- The CRM or tracking system is incomplete.
- The owner carries too much operational memory.
- Past customers are dormant with no reactivation process.
- Local visibility is not turning into booked conversations.

## Deliverables
Possible audit deliverables include:
- Workflow leak summary
- Ranked opportunity list
- Follow-up gap map
- Quick-win recommendations
- Suggested automation path
- Optional implementation proposal
- Weekly recovery and reporting process

## Best Fit
Businesses that already have leads, customers, quotes, inquiries, or repeatable admin work, but lack consistent follow-up or clear visibility into open opportunities.

## Not a Fit
- Businesses looking only for a tool demo
- Businesses with no repeatable workflow to improve
- Businesses unwilling to review missed opportunities honestly

## Next Step
Run the audit, rank the leaks, choose the easiest recovery opportunity, and propose a practical implementation path if there is a clear fit.
