# Workflow Leak Audit Offer

## One-line offer
A practical audit that finds where local businesses are losing follow-up, handoffs, and revenue already earned inside their existing workflows.

## Target problem
Many businesses do not need more leads first. They need to stop leaking the opportunities they already have.

Common leaks include:
- New inquiries that do not get fast follow-up.
- Quotes or estimates that are sent once and forgotten.
- Jobs that are completed without review requests, referrals, or next-step offers.
- Customer information spread across inboxes, texts, spreadsheets, calendars, and CRMs.
- Owners manually remembering what the workflow should have handled.

## Promise
Show the owner where workflow leaks are happening, rank the highest-value fixes, and identify practical AI-assisted improvements without forcing a major software rebuild.

## Deliverables
- Workflow leak map.
- Ranked list of missed-opportunity points.
- Practical recommendations for follow-up, handoffs, and visibility.
- Suggested AI operating-layer improvements.
- Next-step implementation options.

## Boundaries
- This is an audit, not a full automation build unless separately scoped.
- No guaranteed revenue claims.
- No fabricated benchmark data.
