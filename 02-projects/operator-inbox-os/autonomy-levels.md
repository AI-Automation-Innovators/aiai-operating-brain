# Operator Inbox OS Autonomy Levels

## Purpose
Defines how much autonomy AI tools may have in Operator Inbox OS.

## Current Status
Parked / future-facing. These autonomy levels are guardrails, not implementation approval.

## Level 0: Reference Only
AI can read approved source material and answer questions. It cannot draft, decide, send, update, or trigger anything.

## Level 1: Summarize and Flag
AI can summarize inbox items, identify missing information, and flag possible next steps for human review.

## Level 2: Draft for Approval
AI can draft replies, task notes, or follow-up recommendations, but a human must approve before anything is sent or changed.

## Level 3: Human-Approved Action
AI can assist with an approved action after a human confirms scope, wording, destination, and timing.

## Level 4: Limited Automation
Not approved. Any limited automation would require separate written approval, clear scope, testing, rollback rules, and human oversight.

## Never Autonomous Without Approval
- External messages.
- Pricing, discounts, refunds, or terms.
- Sensitive customer situations.
- Legal, medical, financial, or compliance-sensitive content.
- Data deletion or migration.
- Account access changes.
- Public claims, testimonials, or proof statements.

## Update Rules
Update only when Kevin explicitly changes autonomy boundaries.
