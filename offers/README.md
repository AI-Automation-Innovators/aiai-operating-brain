# Offers

This folder holds current offer descriptions and delivery notes.

## Primary offer: Workflow Leak Audit

The Workflow Leak Audit is a practical diagnostic for finding where work slows down, gets repeated, falls through handoffs, or creates missed follow-up. It should identify clear automation opportunities without promising unsupported results.

## Offer language rules

- Be specific about the problem being diagnosed.
- Avoid hype and inflated transformation language.
- Do not promise savings, revenue growth, or time recovery unless proof exists in `proof/`.
- Keep scope clear enough that a human or AI agent can reuse it consistently.

## What belongs here

- Offer descriptions.
- Intake questions.
- Delivery checklists.
- Scope notes and exclusions.

## What does not belong here

- Verified outcomes or case studies; use `proof/`.
- General business positioning; use `business-context/`.
