# Proof and Examples

This folder holds evidence-backed examples, artifacts, screenshots, case-study drafts, and reusable proof.

## Evidence rules

- Do not invent clients, dates, numbers, testimonials, screenshots, or outcomes.
- Label drafts clearly.
- Separate raw artifacts from polished case-study copy when both exist.
- If a claim cannot be supported by an artifact or approved note, do not present it as proof.

## What belongs here

- Real examples of work performed.
- Screenshots or artifacts that support claims.
- Case-study drafts with clear evidence notes.
- Before/after process descriptions when accurate and approved.

## What does not belong here

- Offer positioning; use `offers/`.
- General business context; use `business-context/`.
- Active task tracking; use `projects/`.
