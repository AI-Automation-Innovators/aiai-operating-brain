# Best Emails

Use this file to store outreach emails, follow-ups, and relationship-building messages that are worth reusing or learning from.

Keep entries practical and tied to real business context. Do not invent prospects, replies, or results.

## Email Types

- Chamber introduction
- Local business outreach
- Workflow Leak Audit offer
- Follow-up after no response
- Follow-up after call
- Event invite
- Partnership outreach
- Referral request

## Update Rules

- Add only useful examples.
- Date entries when possible.
- Capture why the example worked.
- Keep proof factual.
- Do not exaggerate.
- Remove weak examples over time.
- Keep this folder curated.
