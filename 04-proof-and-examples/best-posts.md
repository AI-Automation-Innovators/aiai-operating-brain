# Best Posts

## Purpose

Collection of strong public posts for reuse and reference.

## What belongs here

Approved posts, excerpts, links, performance notes, and why they matter.

## What not to include

Posts not approved for reuse, invented engagement metrics, or private material.

## Example categories

- Workflow leaks and follow-up recovery.
- AI as an operating layer, not a novelty tool.
- Operator inbox, handoff, and accountability systems.
- Client education posts that explain the cost of manual coordination.
