## Current primary offer: Workflow Leak Audit

### Status

Current primary offer.

### Description

A structured audit that identifies where a business is losing opportunities through missed follow-up, manual work, weak intake, unclear ownership, and process gaps that could be improved with AI automation.

### Who it helps

Small and local service businesses with repeatable customer workflows, especially when leads, estimates, appointments, customer communication, or follow-ups are spread across inboxes, texts, spreadsheets, calendars, CRMs, or staff memory.

### Problems addressed

- New inquiries that do not get fast follow-up.
- Quotes, estimates, or proposals that are sent once and forgotten.
- Handoffs where ownership or next steps are unclear.
- Manual bottlenecks that depend on the owner or staff remembering what should happen.
- Customer recovery gaps after no-shows, cancellations, stale quotes, or completed jobs.
- Visibility gaps where important work is hidden across tools.

### Positioning

The audit starts with the workflow, not AI for its own sake. AI automation is considered only when it can support follow-up, visibility, handoffs, accountability, or consistency.

### Known boundaries

- This is an audit, not a full automation build unless separately scoped.
- No guaranteed revenue claims.
- No fabricated benchmark data.
- No invented client proof, testimonials, or results.
