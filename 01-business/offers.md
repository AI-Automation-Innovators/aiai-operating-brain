### Workflow Leak Audit
- Status: Draft / developing unless Kevin confirms active status.
- Audience: Central Texas local service businesses and operators with leads, customers, jobs, appointments, inquiries, or follow-ups moving through repeatable workflows.
- Problem: Missed opportunity caused by follow-up gaps, handoff gaps, customer recovery gaps, and limited visibility across existing tools and workflows.
- Promise: Show where workflow leaks are happening, rank the highest-value fixes, and identify practical AI-assisted improvements without forcing a major software rebuild.
- Deliverables:
  - Workflow leak map.
  - Ranked list of missed-opportunity points.
  - Practical recommendations for follow-up, handoffs, and visibility.
  - Suggested AI operating-layer improvements.
  - Next-step implementation options.
- Boundaries:
  - This is an audit, not a full automation build unless separately scoped.
  - No guaranteed revenue claims.
  - No fabricated benchmark data.
  - Pricing, timeline, and final report format are `TBD` until confirmed by Kevin.
