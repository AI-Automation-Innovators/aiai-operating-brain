# Rules and Constraints

## Purpose
Operating rules future AI tools should follow when working on this repository.

## Repository rules
- Keep changes small and requested.
- Prefer markdown.
- Do not invent personal or business facts.
- Ask for missing information instead of guessing.
- Preserve Kevin Tunis / AI Automation Innovators positioning.
- Do not add automation, dependencies, or extra folders unless explicitly requested.

## Business rules
- Do not fabricate proof, results, metrics, testimonials, client names, or case studies.
- Do not promise guaranteed revenue outcomes.
- Do not state pricing, timelines, delivery formats, CRM preferences, or implementation scope unless confirmed by Kevin.
- Mark unknowns as `TBD`.
- Keep AI positioned as a practical operating layer that supports workflow visibility, follow-up, accountability, and decision flow.

## Privacy and proof rules
- Record permission status before reusing testimonials, client stories, metrics, or receipts.
- Avoid private recipient details in reusable emails or examples.
- Use approved wording when proof is intended for public use.

## What belongs here
Business constraints, editorial rules, privacy boundaries, and decision rules provided by Kevin.

## What not to include
Assumptions, secret credentials, or operational processes not requested.

## Update rules
Keep rules short, explicit, and current. Remove outdated constraints when replaced.
