# About AI Automation Innovators

## Purpose
Core business context for AI Automation Innovators.

## What AI Automation Innovators is
AI Automation Innovators is a practical, workflow-first AI automation business led by Kevin Tunis.

The business focuses on finding where day-to-day operations lose opportunities through missed follow-up, manual work, unclear ownership, weak intake, handoff gaps, and limited visibility.

## Who it serves
AI Automation Innovators serves small and local service businesses with repeatable customer workflows.

Strong-fit businesses are usually dealing with leads, customers, jobs, estimates, appointments, inquiries, or follow-ups across tools like inboxes, texts, spreadsheets, calendars, CRMs, or staff memory.

## Problems it focuses on
- Missed or slow follow-up.
- Quotes, estimates, or proposals that are not consistently tracked.
- Handoffs where ownership or next steps are unclear.
- Customer recovery gaps after no-shows, cancellations, stale quotes, or completed jobs.
- Important work hidden across disconnected tools or individual memory.
- Manual bottlenecks that make the owner or staff carry the process by hand.

## Why workflow leaks matter
Workflow leaks matter because businesses can lose opportunities they already earned attention from. More leads do not help much if current inquiries, estimates, customers, and follow-ups are delayed, dropped, or hidden.

The operating question is not "What AI tool should be added first?" It is "Where is the workflow breaking, and what practical improvement would make the business more consistent?"

## How the business talks about AI automation
AI Automation Innovators talks about AI as an operating layer, not a novelty tool.

AI should support clearer follow-up, better visibility, stronger handoffs, and more consistent execution. Tool changes should only be recommended when they solve the workflow problem.
